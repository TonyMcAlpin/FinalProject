This converter can be used for a variety of units related to construction. In the construction world we are always converting units for ordering materials and issuing payments for completed work. This page will make your life easier since you will no longer have to memorize or look up conversion rates.

Example: A client is paid by square yard for the amount of top soil they install. Typically this would be measured in square feet where the material was installed. If the field measurements were 250 square feet, you would enter 250 into the first text box below, and then select square feet in the box labeled “Select Your Starting Unit”. Then select square yards in the last box labeled “Select Your Starting Unit”. Upon selecting square yards, the conversion will automatically happen, and the number 27.778 will appear in the third box.
