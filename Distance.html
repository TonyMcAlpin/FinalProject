This converter can be used for very long distances such as miles, or even very small distances like inches. Sometimes in life we have a unit such as miles and we need to know how many kilometers it equates to. In America we typically only use miles, but in my travels to other countries I have often come across distances in kilometers. This site will make the connection between these units very simple for you.


Example: Someone tells you that a store is 3 kilometers away. You are not familiar with kilometers, and you want to know if this is a walkable distance for you. To find out how many miles this is, enter 3 into the first text box below, and then select kilometers in the box labeled “Select Your Starting Unit”. Then select miles in the last box labeled “Select Your Starting Unit”. Upon selecting miles, the conversion will automatically happen, and the number 1.86 will appear in the third box.
